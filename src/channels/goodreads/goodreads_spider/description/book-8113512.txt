8113512:::::Poison:::::Sixteen-year-old Kyra, a highly-skilled potions master, is the only one who knows her kingdom is on the verge of destruction which means she s the only one who can save it. Faced with no other choice, Kyra decides to do what she does best: poison the kingdom s future ruler, who also happens to be her former best friend. But, for the first time ever, her poisoned dart . . . misses. Now a fugitive instead of a hero, Kyra is caught in a game of hide-and-seek with the king s army and her potioner ex-boyfriend, Hal. At least she s not alone. She s armed with her vital potions, a too-cute pig, and Fred, the charming adventurer she can t stop thinking about. Kyra is determined to get herself a second chance (at murder), but will she be able to find and defeat the princess before Hal and the army find her? Kyra is not your typical murderer, and she s certainly no damsel-in-distress she s the lovable and quick-witted hero of this romantic novel that has all the right ingredients to make teen girls swoon.
