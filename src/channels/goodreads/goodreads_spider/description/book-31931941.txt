31931941:::::Eliza and Her Monsters:::::Her story is a phenomenon. Her life is a disaster. In the real world, Eliza Mirk is shy, weird, and friendless. Online, she s LadyConstellation, the anonymous creator of the wildly popular webcomic Monstrous Sea . Eliza can t imagine enjoying the real world as much as she loves the online one, and she has no desire to try. Then Wallace Warland, Monstrous Sea s biggest fanfiction writer, transfers to her school. Wallace thinks Eliza is just another fan, and as he draws her out of her shell, she begins to wonder if a life offline might be worthwhile. But when Eliza s secret is accidentally shared with the world, everything she s built her story, her relationship with Wallace, and even her sanity begins to fall apart.
