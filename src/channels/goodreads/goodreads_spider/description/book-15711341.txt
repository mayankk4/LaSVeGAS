15711341:::::Written in Red:::::No one creates realms like New York Times bestselling author Anne Bishop. Now in a thrilling new fantasy series, enter a world inhabited by the Others, unearthly entities vampires and shape-shifters among them who rule the Earth and whose prey are humans. As a cassandra sangue , or blood prophet, Meg Corbyn can see the future when her skin is cut a gift that feels more like a curse. Meg s Controller keeps her enslaved so he can have full access to her visions. But when she escapes, the only safe place Meg can hide is at the Lakeside Courtyard a business district operated by the Others. Shape-shifter Simon Wolfgard is reluctant to hire the stranger who inquires about the Human Liaison job. First, he senses she s keeping a secret, and second, she doesn t smell like human prey. Yet a stronger instinct propels him to give Meg the job. And when he learns the truth about Meg and that she s wanted by the government, he ll have to decide if she s worth the fight between humans and the Others that will surely follow.
