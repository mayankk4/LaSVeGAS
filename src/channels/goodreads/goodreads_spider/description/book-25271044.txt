25271044:::::Ishtar's Odyssey: A Storybook for Advent:::::A new Advent family devotional from the author of Jotham s Journey! Fourth in a set of Advent adventure books for families, Ishtar s Odyssey follows the ten-year-old son of a Persian wise man as their caravan follows a star across the desert. Ishtar would just as soon stay in the comfort of the palace, but slowly he learns that there s much to see, do, and learn in this world that can t be experienced in school. He eventually meets Jotham, Bartholomew, and Tabitha as he follows his father and uncles in their search for a newborn king.
