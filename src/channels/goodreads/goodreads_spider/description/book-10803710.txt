10803710:::::The Alloy of Law:::::Centuries after the Mistborn trilogy, Scadrial is on the verge of modernity - railroads, electric street lights, and skyscrapers. After 20 years in the dusty Roughs, in the city of Elendel, the new head of a noble house may need to keep his guns.
