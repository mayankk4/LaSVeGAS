256008:::::Lonesome Dove:::::A love story, an adventure, and an epic of the frontier, Larry McMurtry s Pulitzer Prize winning classic, Lonesome Dove , the third book in the Lonesome Dove tetralogy, is the grandest novel ever written about the last defiant wilderness of America. Journey to the dusty little Texas town of Lonesome Dove and meet an unforgettable assortment of heroes and outlaws, whores and ladies, Indians and settlers. Richly authentic, beautifully written, always dramatic, Lonesome Dove is a book to make us laugh, weep, dream, and remember. Series in order of publication: Lonesome Dove (1985) Streets of Laredo (1993) Dead Man's Walk (1995) Comanche Moon (1997) Series in order of internal chronology: Dead Man's Walk set in the early 1840s Comanche Moon set in the 1850 60s Lonesome Dove set in mid-to-late 1870s Streets of Laredo set in the early 1890s
