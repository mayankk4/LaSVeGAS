29470648:::::If I Was Your Girl:::::Selected as the launch title for the Zoella Book Club. 'Important and brave. Read this wonderful book, just read it.' -- Jennifer Niven, author of All the Bright Places Amanda Hardy is the new girl at school. Like everyone else, all she wants is to make friends and fit in. But Amanda is holding back. Even from Grant, the guy she's falling in love with. Amanda has a secret. At her old school, she used to be called Andrew. And secrets always have a way of getting out. A book about loving yourself and being loved for who you really are.
