33020060:::::Conspiracy of Silence:::::Four years after a tragic mission decimated his career and his team, Cole Tox Russell is persona non grata to the United States. And that s fine he just wants to be left alone. But when a dormant, centuries-old disease is unleashed at an archaeological dig where three Bronze-era censers are found, Tox is lured back into action. Partnered with an archaeologist and an FBI agent who s an expert in deception, Tox and his team are pitted against a secret society, a plague dubbed the New Black Death, and a revered codex which may hold the key to stopping the deadly outbreak.
