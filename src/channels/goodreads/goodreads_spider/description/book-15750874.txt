15750874:::::The Glass Arrow:::::The Handmaid s Tale meets Blood Red Road in Glass Arrow , the story of Aya, who lives with a small group of women on the run from the men who hunt them, men who want to auction off breeding rights to the highest bidder. In a world where females are scarce and are hunted, then bought and sold at market for their breeding rights, 15-year old Aya has learned how to hide. With a ragtag bunch of other women and girls, she has successfully avoided capture and eked out a nomadic but free existence in the mountains. But when Aya s luck runs out and she s caught by a group of businessmen on a hunting expedition, fighting to survive takes on a whole new meaning.
