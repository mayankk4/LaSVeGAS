21535019:::::The Long Haul:::::A family road trip is supposed to be a lot of fun . . . unless, of course, you re the Heffleys. The journey starts off full of promise, then quickly takes several wrong turns. Gas station bathrooms, crazed seagulls, a fender bender, and a runaway pig not exactly Greg Heffley s idea of a good time. But even the worst road trip can turn into an adventure and this is one the Heffleys won t soon forget.
