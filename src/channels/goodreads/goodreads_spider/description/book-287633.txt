287633:::::Dark Magic:::::They were masters of the darkness, searching through eternity for a mistress of the light... Would she find magic or madness in his dark embrace? Young Savannah Dubrinsky is a mistress of illusion, a world-famous magician capable of mesmerizing millions. But there is one Gregori, the Dark One who holds her in terrifying thrall. Whose cold silver eyes and heated sensuality send shivers of danger, of desire, down her slender spine. With a dark magic all his own, Gregori the implacable hunter, the legendary healer, the most powerful of Carpathian males whispers in Savannah's mind that he is her destiny. That she was born to save his immortal soul. And now, here in New Orleans, the hour has finally come to claim her. To make her completely his. In a ritual as old as time . . . and as inescapable as eternity.
