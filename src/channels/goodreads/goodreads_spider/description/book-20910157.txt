20910157:::::Yes Please:::::In Amy Poehler s highly anticipated first book, Yes Please , she offers up a big juicy stew of personal stories, funny bits on sex and love and friendship and parenthood and real life advice (some useful, some not so much), like when to be funny and when to be serious. Powered by Amy s charming and hilarious, biting yet wise voice, Yes Please is a book full of words to live by.
