12578305:::::The Chaos of Stars:::::Isadora's family is seriously screwed up which comes with the territory when you're the human daughter of the ancient Egyptian gods Isis and Osiris. Isadora is tired of living with crazy relatives who think she's only worthy of a passing glance so when she gets the chance to move to California with her brother, she jumps on it. But her new life comes with plenty of its own dramatic and dangerous complications . . . and Isadora quickly learns there's no such thing as a clean break from family.
