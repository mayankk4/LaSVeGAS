8715032:::::Blood Magic:::::It starts off simply. Draw a circle... place a dead leaf in the center... sprinkle some salt... recite a little Latin... add a drop of blood... Maybe that last part isn t exactly simple. Yet somehow it feels right to Silla Kennicott. And nothing in her life has felt remotely right since her parents horrific deaths. She s willing to do anything to uncover the truth about her family even try a few spells from the mysterious book that arrived on her doorstep ... and spill some blood. The book isn t the only recent arrival in Silla s life. There s Nick Pardee, the new guy next door who may have seen Silla casting a spell. She s not sure what he saw and is afraid to find out. But as they spend more time together, Silla realizes this may not be Nick s first encounter with Blood Magic. Brought together by a combination of fate and chemistry, Silla and Nick can t deny their attraction. And they can t ignore the dark presence lurking nearby waiting to reclaim the book and all its power.
