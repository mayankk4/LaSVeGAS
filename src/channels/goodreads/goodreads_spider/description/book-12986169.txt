12986169:::::Embers and Echoes:::::Fan the flames: A teen goddess fires up her search for love and family in this sequel to Wildefire . Ashline Wilde may have needed school to learn that she is actually a reincarnated goddess, but she s ready to move beyond books. She leaves her California boarding school behind and makes for Miami, where she meets a new group of deities and desperately seeks her sister Rose, the goddess of war. But she s also looking for love because even though her romance with Cole had to be snuffed, Ash is a volcano goddess and she doesn t get burned. This sequel to the edgy and action-packed Wildefire continues a fiery drama on an immortal scale.
