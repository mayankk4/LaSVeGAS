9064899:::::Endlessly:::::Kiersten White s New York Times bestselling Paranormalcy trilogy comes to a breathless conclusion with a signature mix of wit, romance, paranormal creatures, and a truly original heroine. In Endlessly , pink-loving, butt-kicking Evie has way too much on her to-do list. Paranormals are begging her to open a faerie gate so they can leave the human world, something Evie s not sure she has the power to do. The Dark Queen is torturing humans and must be destroyed. On top of all that, Evie s prom is coming up. She s not sure what to wear, and, oh, yeah, her shape-shifting boyfriend, Lend, has been cursed so that he falls into an enchanted sleep whenever he and Evie are in the same room and even Evie s ex-boyfriend, the faerie Reth, can t reverse the dark magic. An epic battle is looming, and the choices Evie makes will determine the fate of whole paranormal world and her own life.
