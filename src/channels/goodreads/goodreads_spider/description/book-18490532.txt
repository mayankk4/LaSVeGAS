18490532:::::The Body in the Woods:::::In this new series told from multiple perspectives, teen members of a search and rescue team discover a dead body in the woods. Alexis, Nick, and Ruby have very different backgrounds: Alexis has spent her life covering for her mom s mental illness, Nick s bravado hides his fear of not being good enough, and Ruby just wants to pursue her eccentric interests in a world that doesn t understand her. When the three teens join Portland County Sheriff s Search and Rescue, they are teamed up to search for a autistic man lost in the woods. What they find instead is a dead body. In a friendship that will be forged in danger, fear, and courage, the three team up to find the girl s killer before he can strike one of their own. This first book in April Henry s Point Last Seen YA mystery series is full of riveting suspense, putting readers in the middle of harrowing rescues and crime scene investigations.
