His story begins here before the homemade goat milk soaps and hand-gathered honeys, before his memoir of the city mouse s move to the country, The Bucolic Plague in I Am Not Myself These Days , with plenty of dishy anecdotes and moments of tragi-camp delight ( WashingtonPost ).
