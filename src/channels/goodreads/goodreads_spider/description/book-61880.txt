61880:::::Memory:::::Forced to abandon his undercover role as leader of the Dendarii Mercenaries, Miles Vorkosigan persuades Emperor Gregor to appoint him Imperial Auditor so he can penetrate Barrayar s intelligence and security operations (ImpSec). Simon Illyan, head of ImpSec and Miles former boss, is failing physically and mentally, and Miles sets out to find out why -- and who, if anyone, is behind Illyan s rapid decline. Library Journal calls Miles one of the genre s most enterprising and engaging heroes . A Hugo and Nebula Award finalist.
