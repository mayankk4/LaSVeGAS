8697204:::::Captured by the Highlander:::::From bestselling author Julianne MacLean comes the fiery saga of three Highland warriors sworn to protect their homeland and fight for the women they love... The Enemy in Her Bed Lady Amelia Sutherland would rather die than surrender to a man like Duncan MacLean. He is the fiercest warrior of his clan her people's sworn enemy and tonight he is standing over her bed. Eyes blazing, muscles taut, and battle axe gleaming, MacLean has come to kill Amelia's fianc . But once he sees the lovely, innocent Amelia, he decides to take her instead. The Lover in His Arms Stealing the young bride-to-be is the perfect revenge against the man who murdered Duncan's one true love. But Lady Amelia turns out to be more than a pawn of vengeance and war. This brave, beautiful woman touches something deep in Duncan's soul that is even more powerful than a warrior's fury. But when Amelia begins to fall in love with her captor and surrenders in his arms the real battle begins.
