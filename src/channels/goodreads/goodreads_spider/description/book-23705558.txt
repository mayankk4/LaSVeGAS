23705558:::::Love After Dust:::::Humans had known that the world might come to an end at some point. But in the end what had come calling had been more terrifying, more surprising, and more devastating, then anyone had dreamed. There was no solution. No fix. Just panic. Panic and death. Ashes to ashes, Dust to Dust. The dreams were becoming more vivid as time went on. Now, as Ryan struggled on in this world as one of but a handful of survivors, he had begun questioning what was real and what was dream. Either way, Julia was a huge part of his life now, and he kept living for her.
