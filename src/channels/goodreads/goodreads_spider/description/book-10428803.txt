10428803:::::A Rogue by Any Other Name:::::What a scoundrel wants, a scoundrel gets... A decade ago, the Marquess of Bourne was cast from society with nothing but his title. Now a partner in London s most exclusive gaming hell, the cold, ruthless Bourne will do whatever it takes to regain his inheritance including marrying perfect, proper Lady Penelope Marbury. A broken engagement and years of disappointing courtships have left Penelope with little interest in a quiet, comfortable marriage, and a longing for something more. How lucky that her new husband has access to such unexplored pleasures. Bourne may be a prince of London s underworld, but he vows to keep Penelope untouched by its wickedness a challenge indeed as the lady discovers her own desires, and her willingness to wager anything for them... even her heart.
