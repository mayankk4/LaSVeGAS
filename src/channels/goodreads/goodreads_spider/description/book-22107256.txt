22107256:::::Deep Redemption:::::ONLY THROUGH BLOOD CAN THE DEEPEST REDEMPTION BE FOUND He was born to ascend. He was destined to lead. He was never meant to fail. Prophet Cain lies broken and defeated on the floor of his cell. A cell he has been thrown into by the one person who was supposed to stand by his side until the end. His flesh and blood, his only companion. His beloved twin brother. Cain has sacrificed everything for his people. He left the safety and security of his life of study to infiltrate the infamous Hades Hangmen. He lived with them, rode with them, and then betrayed them. All in the name of a faith he can now feel slipping away from him. Like everything else he once held dear But then his isolation is broken. Another prisoner is brought to the cell next to his. A woman fighting her own demons and fears. A woman who Cain understands needs him, as he needs her. As the connection between them grows, so does Cain s resolve. He will right the wrongs he has done. He will atone for the evil acts carried out in his name. He will save the one he loves Even if the battle takes him to the gates of hell itself. Contemporary Dark Romance.
