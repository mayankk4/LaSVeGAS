22727454:::::Delta: Retribution:::::Fallen Navy SEAL and Titan Group s Delta recruit Trace Reeves wants nothing more than a one-night stand to forget that his twin brother was killed-in-action. Delta: Retribution is the first title in the new Delta series, a spin off from the Titan series. This novella was available in the Hot Alpha SEALs: Military Romance megaset.
