9999795:::::Venom:::::Cassandra Caravello is one of Renaissance Venice s lucky elite: with elegant gowns, sparkling jewels, her own lady s maid, and a wealthy fianc , she has everything a girl could desire. Yet ever since her parents death, Cassandra has felt trapped, alone in a city of water, where the dark and labyrinthine canals whisper of escape. When Cass stumbles upon a murdered woman practically in her own backyard she s drawn into a dangerous world of courtesans, killers, and secret societies. Soon, she finds herself falling for Falco, a mysterious artist with a mischievous grin... and a spectacular skill for trouble. Can Cassandra find the murderer, before he finds her? And will she stay true to her fianc , or succumb to her uncontrollable feelings for Falco? Beauty, love, romance, and mystery weave together in a stunning novel that s as seductive and surprising as the city of Venice itself.
