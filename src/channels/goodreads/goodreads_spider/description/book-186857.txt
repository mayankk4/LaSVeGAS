186857:::::Lost and Found:::::The author of Smoke in Mirrors and Soft Focus presents a sexy, irresistible story of two people searching for secrets and finding each other Cady Briggs is useful to Mack Easton. Her expertise in art and antiques helps his low-profile company, Lost and Found, find missing treasures for high-paying clients. But Cady knows that being useful to a client is one thing and being used is another. So no matter how alluring she finds Mack, she plans to keep business and pleasure entirely separate. But then a sudden tragedy puts Cady in charge of Chatelaine s, her family s prestigious art and antiques gallery. Suddenly the roles are reversed, as strange developments at Chatelaine s lead Cady to ask for help from none other than Mack Easton. And instead of tracking down missing masterpieces together, they ll be hunting for a killer
