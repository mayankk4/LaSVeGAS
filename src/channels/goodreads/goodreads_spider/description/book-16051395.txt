16051395:::::When Summer Ends:::::Chloe Henderson has never been one to break the rules or push the boundaries but during her summer break, she and her friends use fake IDs to sneak into a club. It s there that she meets handsome and alluring bartender, Will Morris. The pair hit it off immediately, growing closer with every passing day. But as with every summer, it has to end sometime. Chloe s senior year approaches and there s a shock in store when she returns to do her final year at school. Will is there too, but he s not one of her fellow students A tale of forbidden love, broken hearts, friendship, and rivalry.
