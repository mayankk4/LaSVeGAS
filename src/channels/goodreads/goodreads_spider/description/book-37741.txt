37741:::::Tales of a Fourth Grade Nothing:::::Life with his little brother, Fudge, makes Peter Hatcher feel like a fourth grade nothing. Whether Fudge is throwing a temper tantrum in a shoe store, smearing mashed potatoes on the walls at Hamburger Heaven, or trying to fly, he s never far from trouble. He s an almost three-year-old terror who gets away with everything, and Peter s had it up to here!When Fudge walks off with Dribble, Peter s pet turtle, it s the last straw. Peter has put up with Fudge for too long. Way too long! How can he get his parents to pay attention to him for a change?
