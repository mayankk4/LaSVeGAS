But when Avery Morgansten comes crashing into his life literally he finally meets the one person who can resist his soulful baby blues. But Cam s not ready to give up he can t get this feisty and intriguing girl out of his head. Avery has secrets, secrets that keep her from admitting the feelings Cam knows she has for him.
