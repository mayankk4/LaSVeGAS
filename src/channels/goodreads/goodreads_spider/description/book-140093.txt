Death comes calling on small-town librarian Aurora Teagarden in this entertaining mystery from #1 New York Times bestselling author Charlaine Harris. Going to two weddings one of a former lover and a funeral for a member of her disbanded crime study club keeps Aurora Roe Teagarden quite busy for a few months. Unfortunately, her personal life seems to be at a standstill until her fortunes unexpectedly change. After the funeral Roe learns that Jane Engle, the deceased, has named her as heir to a rather substantial estate, which includes money, jewelry, and a house complete with a skull hidden in a window seat. Knowing Jane, Roe concludes that the elderly woman has purposely left her a murder to solve. So she must identify the victim and figure out which one of Jane s ordinary-seeming neighbors is a murderer without putting herself in deadly danger...
