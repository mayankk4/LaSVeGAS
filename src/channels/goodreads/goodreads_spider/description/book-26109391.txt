Ambitious Lisa desperately wants to get into the second-best psychology program for college (she s being realistic). But is ambition alone enough to get her in? Enter Lisa. But, as Lisa learns more about Sol and he and Clark grow closer and closer, the walls they ve built around themselves start to collapse and their friendships threaten to do the same.
