173502:::::Ruthless Trust: The Ragamuffin's Path to God:::::In his sequel to The Ragamuffin Gospel bestselling author Brennan Manning shows how true and radical trust in God can transform our lives. Manning, beloved author and spiritual teacher, shows us how trust in God can transform our lives and open us up to deeper experiences of grace and love. In Ruthless Trust , he turns his focus from furious love to radical trust, revealing the ways in which trust renews our faith and help us grow.
