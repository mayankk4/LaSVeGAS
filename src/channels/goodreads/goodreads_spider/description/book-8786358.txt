8786358:::::Sacrificial Magic:::::READING, WRITING, AND RAISING THE DEAD When Chess Putnam is ordered by an infamous crime boss who also happens to be her drug dealer to use her powers as a witch to solve a grisly murder involving dark magic, she knows she must rise to the challenge. Adding to the intensity: Chess s boyfriend, Terrible, doesn t trust her, and Lex, the son of a rival crime lord, is trying to reignite the sparks between him and Chess. Plus there s the little matter of Chess s real job as a ghost hunter for the Church of Real Truth, investigating reports of a haunting at a school in the heart of Downside. Someone seems to be taking a crash course in summoning the dead and if Chess doesn t watch her back, she may soon be joining their ranks. As Chess is drawn into a shadowy world of twisted secrets and dark violence, it soon becomes clear that she s not going to emerge from its depths without making the ultimate sacrifice.
