26074181:::::The Crown:::::When Eadlyn became the first princess of Ill a to hold her own Selection, she didn t think she would fall in love with any of her thirty-five suitors. She spent the first few weeks of the competition counting down the days until she could send them all home. But as events at the palace force Eadlyn even further into the spotlight, she realizes that she might not be content remaining alone. Eadlyn still isn t sure she ll find the fairytale ending her parents did twenty years ago. But sometimes the heart has a way of surprising you and soon Eadlyn must make a choice that feels more impossible and more important than she ever imagined.
