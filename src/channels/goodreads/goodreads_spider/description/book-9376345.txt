9376345:::::Bite Club:::::New York Times bestselling author Rachel Caine is "a first-class storyteller" (#1 New York Times bestselling author Charlaine Harris). After discovering that vampires populate her town, college student Claire Danvers knows that the undead just want to live their lives. But someone else wants them to get ready to rumble. There's a new extreme sport getting picked up on the Internet: bare knuckle fights pitting captured vampires against each other or humans. Tracking the remote signal leads Claire accompanied by her friends and frenemies to discover that what started as an online brawl will soon threaten everyone in Morganville...
