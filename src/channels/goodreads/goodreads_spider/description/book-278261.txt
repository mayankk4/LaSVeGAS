278261:::::Every Living Thing:::::For more than twenty years, James Herriot has captivated millions of readers and television viewers with tales of the triumphs, disasters, pride and sometimes heartache that filled his life as a vet in the Yorkshire Dales. Every Living Thing shines with the storytelling magic that has made him a favourite the world over. It also reveals more of the real James Herriot than ever before. Here is a book for all those who find laughter and joy in animals, and who know and understand the magic of wild places and beautiful countryside.
