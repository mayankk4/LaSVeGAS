3217673:::::Catharsis:::::Note: This was a free, serialized story still available for download on the author's website. ---------------- Live the Journey...Experience the Horror...Discover the Truth. Catharsis tells the story of a troubled young woman with special gifts and heavy burdens... In an attempt to escape mysterious forces that may ultimately destroy her, Eve Shelby packs up her little sister, Dez, and embarks on a journey with a group of fellow telepaths. After a car accident leaves one person dead and Dez missing, Eve finds she must come to terms with her unique abilities and personal demons if she's going to survive and find her sister. But you can't always escape what you are...
