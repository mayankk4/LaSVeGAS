20617402:::::Echoes of Scotland Street:::::Shannon MacLeod has always gone for the wrong type of man. After she drifted from one toxic relationship to the next, her last boyfriend gave her a wakeup call in the worst possible way. With her world shattered, she s sworn off men especially those of the bad-boy variety. Cole Walker is exactly the sort that Shannon wants to avoid gorgeous, tattooed, charming, and cocky. But his rough exterior hides a good man who s ready to find the one. He s determined to pull Shannon from her self-imposed solitude and win her heart. As Shannon opens up in the face of Cole s steady devotion, the passion between them ignites to blazing levels. But when Shannon s past comes back to haunt her, her fears may destroy the trust Cole has built between them and tear them apart for good
