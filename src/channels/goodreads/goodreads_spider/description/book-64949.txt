64949:::::The Voice of the Night:::::No one could understand why Colin and Roy were best friends. Colin was so shy; Roy was so popular. Colin was nervous around girls; Roy was a ladies man. Colin was fascinated by Roy and Roy was fascinated with death. Then one day Roy asked his timid friend, You ever killed anything? And from that moment on, the two were bound together in a game too terrifying to imagine and too irresistible to stop. Includes an afterword by the author
