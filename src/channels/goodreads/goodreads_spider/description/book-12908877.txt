12908877:::::Until I Die:::::Kate and Vincent have overcome the odds and at last they are together in Paris, the city of lights and love. As their romance deepens there s one question they can t ignore: How are they supposed to be together if Vincent can t resist sacrificing himself to save others? Although Vincent promises that he ll do whatever it takes to lead a normal life with Kate, will that mean letting innocent people die? When a new and surprising enemy reveals itself, Kate realizes that even more may be at stake and that Vincent s immortality is in jeopardy. In Die for Me, Amy Plum created a captivating paranormal mythology with immortal revenants and a lush Paris setting. Until I Die is poised to thrill readers with more heart-pounding suspense, spellbinding romance, and a cliff-hanger ending that will leave them desperate for the third and final novel in the series.
