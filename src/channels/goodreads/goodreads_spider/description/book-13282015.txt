13282015:::::Wait for Me:::::A 2012 Readers' Choice Awards Finalist in Romantic Suspense! A woman without a past After a tragic accident left her with no memory, Kate Alexander struggled to fit in with a husband and world that didn t feel right. She s had no reason to question what friends and family have told her, not until her husband is suddenly killed and she finds a photo of a young girl in his office. A girl who can t be anyone but a daughter Kate didn t know she had. A man desperate for a reason to live Ryan Harrison lost his wife in a plane crash five years ago. To cope with the pain of her loss, he dedicated himself to his job and to raising their daughter. Now a successful pharmaceutical executive, Ryan has everything a man could want money, fame and power but he d give it all up in a heartbeat for just one more day with the woman he still loves. Two lives about to converge. As Kate begins to dig into a past she doesn t remember, evidence leads her to San Francisco and puts her on the path toward Ryan, a man who sees in her the woman he loved and lost. Kate feels a draw to Ryan, one she can t explain, but is that feeling enough to convince her this is where she s supposed to be? As Ryan and Kate search for answers, they uncover lies long buried, a passion hotter than either expected and a danger that threatens even now when the second chance they ve both been searching for is finally within reach.
