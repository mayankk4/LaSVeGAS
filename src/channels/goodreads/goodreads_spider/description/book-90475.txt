90475:::::Teen Idol:::::High school junior Jenny Greenley is so good at keeping secrets that she's the school newspaper's anonymous advice columnist. She's so good at it that, when hotter-than-hot Hollywood star Luke Striker comes to her small town to research a role, Jenny is the one in charge of keeping his identity under wraps. But Luke doesn't make it easy, and soon everyone the town, the paparazzi, and the tabloids alike know his secret...and Jenny is caught right in the middle of all the chaos.
