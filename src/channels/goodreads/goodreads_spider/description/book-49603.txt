49603:::::The Hollow Chocolate Bunnies of the Apocalypse:::::Toy Town older, bigger, and certainly not wiser. The Old Rich, who have made their millions from the royalties on their world-famous nursery rhymes, are being murdered one by one. A psychopath is on the loose, and he must be stopped at any cost. It s a job for Toy Town s only detective but he s missing, leaving only Eddie Bear, and his bestest friend Jack, to track down the mad killer.
