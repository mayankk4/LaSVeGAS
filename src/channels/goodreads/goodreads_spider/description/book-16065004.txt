16065004:::::Shadows of Self:::::Shadows of Self shows Mistborn s society evolving as technology and magic mix, the economy grows, democracy contends with corruption, and religion becomes a growing cultural force, with four faiths competing for converts. This bustling, optimistic, but still shaky society now faces its first instance of terrorism, crimes intended to stir up labor strife and religious conflict. Wax and Wayne, assisted by the lovely, brilliant Marasi, must unravel the conspiracy before civil strife stops Scadrial s progress in its tracks. Shadows of Self will give fans of The Alloy of Law everything they ve been hoping for and, this being a Brandon Sanderson book, more, much more.
