33236889:::::Franco:::::Franco Genovese is the drummer for world renowned American rock band, Rook. He s got it all. A killer smile. Tattoos. Talent. Razor sharp wit and humor. And a heart as big, and generous, as they come. Life is good. Steady. Uncomplicated. Just the way he likes things. Until one night at an unassuming L.A. bar changes everything. Enter Gemma Hendricks. She s a successful young architect from Northern England with an adorable smile, sarcasm for days, and an unparalleled trusting heart. The attraction is instant. So is their friendship. It s also temporary because they ll both be heading home, thousands of miles apart from each other, in a few days. Or is it? There s something Gemma wants more than anything else. And when Franco propositions her to provide what she s looking for, everything changes. Will it transform friendship into love, or will it be their ruin? Note from the author: Due to strong language and sexual content, this book is recommended for mature audiences.
