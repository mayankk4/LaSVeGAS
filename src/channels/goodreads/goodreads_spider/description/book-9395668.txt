9395668:::::Anya's Ghost:::::Of all the things Anya expected to find at the bottom of an old well, a new friend was not one of them. Especially not a new friend who s been dead for a century. Falling down a well is bad enough, but Anya's normal life might actually be worse. She's embarrassed by her family, self-conscious about her body, and she's pretty much given up on fitting in at school. A new friend--even a ghost--is just what she needs. But Anya's new B.F.F. isn't kidding about the "forever" part...
