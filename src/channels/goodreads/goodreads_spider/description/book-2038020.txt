2038020:::::Tashi:::::In the first book of the much-adored Tashi series, children are introduced to Jack s extraordinary imaginary friend Tashi, a gnome-like character from a place far away. Brave Tashi tells adventurous tales of being sold to a warlord and escaping on a swan. This little hero has to be clever to outsmart giants, ghosts, demons, and witches that stand in his way. Young readers will be captivated by Tashi s tall tales of courage and daring.
