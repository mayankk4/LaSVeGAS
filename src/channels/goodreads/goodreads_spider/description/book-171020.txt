171020:::::A Mango-Shaped Space:::::Mia Winchell appears to be a typical kid, but she's keeping a big secret sounds, numbers, and words have color for her. No one knows, and Mia wants to keep it that way. But when trouble at school finally forces Mia to reveal her secret, she must learn to accept herself and embrace her ability, called synesthesia, a mingling of the senses.
