12963457:::::The Devil's Eyes:::::Wrong side of the tracks that s where Kayla s life has been. Her only saving grace was her sister, Braylin. When Braylin dies, Kayla goes on a mission to avenge her death. Nick is the mysterious stranger with the alluring devil s eyes. He is attractive, powerful, and dangerous. If not for her obsession for revenge, Kayla would never dare go near him. To lure Nick into her trap, she has to become what he needs while trying to fight against her growing desires. On a destructive path, Kayla enters a secret world of underground crime. As Kayla falls deeper into Nick s world, she becomes the target of many enemies, and the only one who can save her is the one she has set out to destroy.
