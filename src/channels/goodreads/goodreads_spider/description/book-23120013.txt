23120013:::::The List:::::An intense look at the rules of high school attraction and the price that s paid for them. It happens every year. A list is posted, and one girl from each grade is chosen as the prettiest, and another is chosen as the ugliest. Nobody knows who makes the list. It almost doesn t matter. The damage is done the minute it goes up. This is the story of eight girls, freshman to senior, pretty and ugly. And it s also the story of how we see ourselves, and how other people see us, and the tangled connection of the two.
