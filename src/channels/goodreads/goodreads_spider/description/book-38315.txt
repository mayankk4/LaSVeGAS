38315:::::Fooled by Randomness: The Hidden Role of Chance in Life and in the Markets:::::Fooled by Randomness is a standalone book in Nassim Nicholas Taleb s landmark Incerto series, an investigation of opacity, luck, uncertainty, probability, human error, risk, and decision-making in a world we don t understand. The other books in the series are The Black Swan, Antifragile, and The Bed of Procrustes .
