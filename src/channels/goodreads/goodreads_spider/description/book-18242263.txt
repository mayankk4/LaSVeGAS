18242263:::::Jealousy:::::When Heather Cooper married Peter, she thought that she had finally found someone who could handle the inseparable bond she shares with her gay best friend, Justin Perrotta. It s only a matter of time, however, before jealousy rears its ugly head and Peter s true feelings emerge. He starts drinking and his erratic behavior threatens their marriage. Burned by an ex-boyfriend, Justin refuses to open his heart to love again. Wild relationships and one night stands leave him lonely and unfulfilled, even though he will not admit it. He finds love when he least expects it, but his fear of commitment threatens to ruin the best thing that has ever happened to him. In this modern day Will and Grace meets Sex and the City , two best friends, a straight woman and a gay man, struggle to find someone to love as much as they love each other.
