4894:::::Who Moved My Cheese?:::::Written by Spencer Johnson, coauthor of The One Minute Manager , this enlightening and amusing story illustrates the vital importance of being able to deal with unexpected change. Who Moved My Cheese ? is often distributed by managers to employees as a motivational tool, but the lessons it teaches can benefit literally anyone, young or old, rich or poor, looking for less stress and more success in every aspect of work and life.
