6250169:::::Unseen Academicals:::::Discworld lives on in Unseen Academicals, the latest novel from Terry Pratchett. Delivering the trademark insight and humor readers the world over have come to expect from the purely funniest English writer since Wodehouse ( Washington Post Book World ), Unseen Academicals focuses on the wizards at Ankh-Morpork s UnseenUniversity, who are reknowned for many things sagacity, magic, and their love of teatime as they attempt to conquer athletics.
